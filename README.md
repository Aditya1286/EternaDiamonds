# Diamond Jewelry Viewer

> A comprehensive showcase platform for displaying available diamond inventory to customers

## Overview

Diamond Jewelry Viewer is an interactive product showcase designed to help customers explore and view the available diamond inventory in real-time. This platform provides a seamless browsing experience for customers to discover diamonds that match their preferences and requirements.

## Features

- **Real-Time Inventory Display** - View current availability of diamonds in stock
- **Detailed Product Information** - Access comprehensive details about each diamond including:
  - Carat weight
  - Cut quality
  - Color grade
  - Clarity rating
  - Certification details
  - Pricing information
- **Interactive Viewer** - Explore diamonds with high-quality images and visualizations
- **Search & Filter Options** - Find diamonds based on specific criteria
- **Availability Status** - Instant updates on stock availability

## Purpose

This viewer serves as a bridge between our diamond inventory and customers, enabling them to:
- Browse available diamonds efficiently
- Make informed purchasing decisions
- Check real-time availability
- Compare different diamonds side-by-side
- Request specific diamonds for purchase or consultation

## Getting Started

### For Customers

1. Browse the diamond collection through the viewer interface
2. Use filters to narrow down options based on your preferences
3. Click on any diamond to view detailed specifications
4. Check availability status in real-time
5. Contact us to inquire about or purchase available diamonds

### For Administrators

1. Update inventory regularly to reflect current stock
2. Upload high-quality images for each diamond
3. Ensure all specifications are accurate and up-to-date
4. Monitor customer inquiries and respond promptly

## Technical Specifications
